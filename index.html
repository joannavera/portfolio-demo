Bio

I’m an artist and designer originally from Bethlehem, Pennsylvania but made a huge move to Austin, Texas in 2020.

I graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design with a BFA in Sequential Art and a minor in Drawing. My passion is to visually create stories and design characters, but I’m eager to use her artistic and illustrative background to create design solutions with UX.

In future projects, I look forward to be able to combine both illustration, storytelling, and UI design all together!
